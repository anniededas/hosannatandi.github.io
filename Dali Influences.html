Salvador Dalí

Influences:

Art styles: Impressionism, Cubism, Futurism, Dada Movement

Dalí also met many influential people on his frequent trips to Paris. Here he met Pablo Picasso, the Spanish painter and sculptor Joan Miró, poet Paul Eluard and painter Rene Magritte. These amazing artists introduced Dalí to the style of Surrealism, which he is most famous for.
